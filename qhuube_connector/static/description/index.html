Qhuube

Qhuube is a VAT compliance platform designed for the coming era of EU digital tax reporting.

The platform provides businesses and software systems with a single interface to automate EU VAT compliance. Today it supports OSS VAT reporting, and it is being built to scale into the ViDA (VAT in the Digital Age) framework that will introduce real-time digital VAT reporting across the European Union.

  • Automate EU OSS VAT reporting
  • Prepare systems for ViDA digital VAT infrastructure
  • One compliance layer across all EU jurisdictions
  • Designed for integration with business software and ERP systems
